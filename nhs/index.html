The MHS National Honor Society wants to help you with all your high school needs.

About

Come find us in lecture hall 3!

We will be in lecture hall 3 from 2:45-3:45 from Mondays through Thursdays every week*. We will have snacks and music.

*Due to NHS meetings, sessions on Monday may start later than 3:00.

Goals

Tutoring/Homework Help

We are happy to help in all school subjects. Come with questions or homework

Networking

We’ll help you with community service, jobs, internships, portfolio/résumé assistance, and more!

Advice

Ask us anything! We're here to provide the tips and tricks to figure out MHS.
