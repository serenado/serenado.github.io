Mauna Kea

I am at the tallest mountain in the world, which rises from the Pacific Ocean floor about 18,000 feet up to sea level, then another 13,796 feet above sea level. It's on the Big Island of Hawaii and its name is Mauna Kea, it is a dormant volcana. If I am standing on the Big Island 16,000 feet away from the Mauna Kea, looking at the top of the mountain, what is the angle of elevation? How long is my line of site?

Taipei 101

I am seeing the one of the tallest buildings in the world: Taipei 101. Let x equal the angle of elevation when I look up to the top of the building. I know that tan x= 3.34 and the line of sight from me to the top of the tower is 1,744 feet long. How tall is Tapiei 101?

Kingda Ka

I am visiting the tallest roller coaster in the US. It is called the Kingda Ka, located at Six Flags Great Adventure in Jackson Township, New Jersey. Since I'm so little, I can only see that the minimem height the roller coaster reaches in 39 feet. But the angle formed by my lines of sight between the minimum and aximum heights of the roller coaster is 52 degrees and I am standing 255 feet away from the roller coaster.
